    Donate items to our shops

    Donate pre-loved items and corporate stock through our stores and online marketplaces to help raise funds for world-class cancer research.

    Find your nearest shop

    How to donate your pre-loved items

    We have almost 600 shops and superstores across the UK all needing stock. Find out how to drop off items at your nearest Cancer Research UK shop, or arrange a home, business or corporate partner collection.

    Our shops and superstores are always in need of good quality donations, such as:

    • clothing and footwear

    • jewellery and accessories

    • homeware and kitchenware

    • antiques and collectables

    • toys

    • leisure and outdoor items

    • furniture and electricals (in select shops and superstores)

    Occasionally, due to stock supply and demand, there may be situations where we arent able to accept items from certain categories such as childrens clothing, books and media, which is at the discretion of our transport and shop teams.

    We appreciate all donations, but we are unable to accept items which we cannot sell and would require us to pay for disposal. This includes anything dirty, damaged or with missing parts.

    Items we cant accept

    While we appreciate all donations, there are some we cannot sell and we have to pay for their disposal.

    Read a list of items we cant accept

    Corporate donations

    Donate your end-of-line or unsold stock and samples to us as a cost-effective way to clear storage space. Or you can set up a donation station in your office.

    Learn about corporate donations

    High-value items

    If you have high-value items you wish to donate, please get in touch via the email link below.

    Please include these details in your email to us:

    • Contact name

    • Full address

    • Telephone number

    • Description of items (brand, model, condition etc.)

    • Estimated value and photos (if possible)

    Contact us here

    Set up a Donation Station

    A Donation Station is a drop-off point where people can bring their quality pre-loved items todonate. You can then take your donation bags to your nearest shop, during opening hours. This saves us money on collection costs.

    How to set up a Donation Station

    Arrange a furniture collection

    Some of our superstores take good-quality furniture donations. You can call or visit your local superstore who can take a booking over the phone or in person. Or you can book online if it's easier.

    Book a furniture collection

    Arrange a home collection

    We can collect from you if you're donating 25 or more bags and live within a 20-mile radius of any of our shops and Superstores.

    We accept bags which are of standard 'bag for life' size (450 x 380 x 180mm) or larger than this (no heavier than 7kg). You can also donate your items in boxes if you prefer.

    Please ensure all donated items are securely tied up in bags, or protected in boxes or other appropriate packaging, ready for the driver to collect before submitting a collection request

    Book a home collection

    Retail Gift Aid

    At no extra cost to you we can claim Gift Aid on the items you donate to our shops. Thats an extra 25p for every 1 sold towards funding our vital research.

    Learn more about Gift Aid
