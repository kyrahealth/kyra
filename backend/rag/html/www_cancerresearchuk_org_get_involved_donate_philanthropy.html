    Philanthropy and major gifts

    Philanthropy is funding some of our greatest research. If you can support us with a gift of over £10,000, you'll have a profound impact on cancer prevention, diagnosis and treatment.

    More Research, Less Cancer

    Our campaign aims to raise £400m to:

    • power wide-ranging scientific discovery

    • unite behind shared challenges

    • find and fund future leaders in cancer research

    • support innovation that translates into front-line therapies and treatments

    From pledging a multi-year gift to donating shares or hosting an event, there are many ways you can support more cutting-edge science.

    • Offer us your time and expertise

    • Give through a trust or foundation

    • Leave a gift in your Will

    • Make a donation online
