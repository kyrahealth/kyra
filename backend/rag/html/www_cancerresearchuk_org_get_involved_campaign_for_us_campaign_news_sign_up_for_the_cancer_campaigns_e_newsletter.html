Campaign for change

Our community of campaigners save lives through political action every day. Every action they take brings us one step closer to beating cancer.

They've helped secure additional funding for cancer tests and radiotherapy machines; protected young people from the harms of tobacco through the introduction of plain, standardised cigarette packaging; and put children's obesity on the political agenda. 

You can be part of our next success by becoming an e-campaigner. 

Each month you'll receive an email with a quick and easy online action you can take to influence the Government on one of our policy priorities. For example, you might email your MP about the importance of diagnosing cancer early to give people the best chance of survival or add your name to a quick and easy petition.

Please let us know your details in the form to start receiving your monthly e-campaigner action. You'll need to opt-in to our monthly emails by ticking the 'Yes' box on the form.
