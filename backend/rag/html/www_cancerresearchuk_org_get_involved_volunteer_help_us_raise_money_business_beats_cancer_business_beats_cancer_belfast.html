        Business Beats Cancer Belfast

        Business Beats Cancer Belfast brings together business leaders to raise vital funds for Cancer Research UK. The Business Beats Cancer Belfast board is one of a growing network of boards across the UK. Each board organises an exciting annual fundraising event that brings together businesses in its local area to beat cancer.
